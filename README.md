## <u>**Conclusion**</u>

* Tier 3 locations lead sales ($472K), showing strong demand.
* Type 1 outlets generate most revenue ($787K), highlighting efficiency.
* Fruits, Snacks, and Frozen Foods are top categories; low-fat items sell well.
* Sales peaked in 2018 ($205K) and slightly declined afterward.
* Medium-sized outlets perform steadily.
* Growth opportunities: focus on Tier 3 markets, expand top categories, and optimize pricing strategies.
